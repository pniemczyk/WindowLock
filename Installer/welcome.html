WindowLock

Your windows. Your monitors. Your layout. Every single time.

You know the feeling. You open your laptop, connect your monitors, and every window is piled on top of each other. That carefully arranged multi-monitor setup you spent ten minutes perfecting? Gone. macOS just doesn't care.

WindowLock fixes this. It's a tiny, silent menu bar app that remembers where every window belongs and puts it back. After sleep, after restart, after your cat unplugs your monitor. Small. Simple. Set it and forget it.

This installer will:

  • Install WindowLock.app in /Applications
  • Configure it to start automatically on login
  • Launch WindowLock immediately
Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later.
