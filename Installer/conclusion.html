You're all set!

WindowLock is now running in your menu bar. Look for the window icon in the top-right of your screen. It's already capturing your window positions.

One last thing: grant Accessibility permission

WindowLock needs Accessibility access to move and resize your windows. Without it, capturing works but restoring doesn't.

  1. Open System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility
  2. Find WindowLock in the list and enable the toggle
  3. If it's not listed, click + and add /Applications/WindowLock.app

You can also get there from the WindowLock menu: Configuration > Permissions.

To uninstall: Click the WindowLock menu bar icon > Configuration > Uninstall WindowLock.
